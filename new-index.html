Welcome, you've found my home on the internet. My name is Christopher Strelioff, but please call me Chris.

science I am a scientist by training, with degrees in physics, but I have worked on problems in statistical inference, information theory, ecology, evolution, epidemiology and more-- check out my publications if you're interested.

programming I also love the application of mathematics and programming to problems of all types. Currently I tackle most of my (computational) problems with Python and Ubuntu Linux-- see my sandbox blog for more information.

arts I have a passion for the arts including music, film, books, painting, prints, etc. with a special place in my heart for music. Check out my lastfm profile (link above) and be sure to connect if we have some overlapping interests!

This was my blog at wordpress between 2012 and 2014, covering topics related to using Ubuntu 12.04 for scientific computing. During this time the site has attracted more than 50,000 views and now averages 100 views per day. I know, small potatoes, but it's not bad for a little blog about installing Python, R and vim on Ubuntu 12.04!
