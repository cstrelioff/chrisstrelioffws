about me



I love to learn and to figure things out. By training I am a physicist, but I have worked on projects in many domains. For this work I have developed mathematical and statistical models, typically implemented in python or R.

I now live in Oakland, CA and would like to move into the tech industry as a data scientist or analyst. I know that I will enjoy applying my scientific, statistical, programming and communication skills to new and interesting problems.

Are you looking for someone like me? Don't be shy, drop me a note.
